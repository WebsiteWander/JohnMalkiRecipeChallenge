Slow Cooker Black Bean Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 Red Bell Peppers, cored and chopped
  • 2 Carrots, chopped
  • 5 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 Jalapeno Pepper, seeded and diced
  • 4 Cups of (good quality) Vegetable Broth
  • 4 15 oz. cans of Black Beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1 can of Corn, drained
  • 1 can of Green Chilis (Mild), drained and rinsed
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 2 tsps. of Ground Cumin
  • 2 tsps. of Chili Powder
  • 2 tsps. of Pink Sea Salt
  • 1/2 tsp. of Cayenne

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in slow cooker bowl/Instapot, and stir to combine
  2. Cook on high for 4 hours
  3. Remove the Bay Leaf before eating (if possible)


Zucchini and Rice Stew

Ingredients

  • 8 (medium) Zucchinis, sliced and quartered
  • 1 cup of Brown Rice, cooked
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 28 oz. can of Chopped Canned Tomato, drained
  • 1 6 oz. can of Tomato Paste
  • 2 Cups of (good quality) Vegetable Broth
  • 4 Tbsp. of Lemon Juice
  • 1 tsp. of Pink Sea Salt
  • 1/2 tsp. of Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté the minced Garlic Cloves (for Instapot, Sauté on "Normal" heat setting)
  2. Add all other ingredients
  3. Bring everything to boil (for Instapot, Sauté on "More" heat setting until it starts to boil)
  4. Cover and simmer for 40 minutes (for Instapot, keep lid removed and Slow Cook on "Less" heat setting for 40 minutes (stir occasionally))

  5. (Optional, if too watery) Remove lid and let simmer for longer to let sauce thicken (for Instapot, keep lid removed and use the "Keep Warm" setting)


Paprika Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. of cooked (no peeling necessary) Yukon Gold Potatoes (cut potatoes in half lengthwise and then into 1/4 inch silces. Rinse well to wash off the starch)
  • 1 Large Yellow Onion, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp. and 1/4 tsp. of Pink Sea Salt
  • 1/4 tsp. of Black Pepper
  • 3/4 cups (or 1 1/2 cups) of Water
  • 1/4 tsp. of Sweet Paprika
  • 1 Tbsp. of fresh Chives

Instructions

  1. Sauté onions with 1/4 tsp. of Salt, until nicely browned (approx. 18 min)
  2. Remove onions and set them aside
  3. Put potatoes in a Dutch Oven with 3/4 cup of water and 1/2 tsp. of Salt (for Instapot, put Potatoes in the Streamer Basket, and add 1 1/2 cups to bottom of the pot)
  4. Bring water to boil without the lid, then reduce the heat to Simmer and put on the lid (for Instapot, Pressure Cook for 4 minutes on High Pressure)
  5. After 20 mins, reduce heat to Low (for Instapot, after the Timer beeps, wait 5 minutes then do a Quick Pressure Release)
  6. Mash the Potatoes (for Instapot, put the potatoes in bottom of the pot (with the water), mash them, and stir in 1/2 tsp. of Salt)
  7. Add into the pot the cooked Onions, Paprika, 1/4 tsp. of Salt, and 1/4 tsp. of Pepper, and stir everything together
  8. Add fresh Chives

  9. (Optional) Add extra Salt and Pepper to taste
  10. (Optional) Dress with Sprinkled Paprika and Chives


Red Beans and Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of dry Small Red Beans (NOT KIDNEY BEANS)
  • 1 1/2 cups of dry Brown Rice
  • 1 medium Yellow Onion, diced
  • 1 medium Green (or Red) Bell Pepper, diced
  • 3 stalks of Celery, diced
  • 3 or 4 Garlic Cloves, minced
  • 1 1/2 Tbsps. of Cajun Seasoning (with Sodium) OR 1/2 tsp. of Pink Sea Salt
  • 2 tsps. of Dry Thyme
  • 2 tsps. of Parsley
  • 4 cups of Vegetable Broth
  • 1 1/2 cups of Water
  • 1 medium Lemon, juiced

Instructions

  1. Rinse and sort out any debris from the dry Red Beans and dry Brown Rice, then set them aside
  2. Using the Instapot, press the sauté setting, and wait for it to read "Hot"
  3. Sauté the diced Onions, Celery, and Bell Peppers for 6 minutes
  4. Stir in the Garlic, Cajun Seasoning/Salt, Thyme, and Parsley
  5. Stir for 30 more seconds, stirring frequently to prevent burning
  6. Pour in 3 Tbsps. of the total Vegetable Broth to deglaze the pot and remove any stuck bits
  7. Add the remaining ingredients (the remaining Vegetable Broth, Water, dried Red Beans, and dried Brown Rice)
  8. Do not stir
  9. Close the lid and Pressure Cook on "High Pressure" for 30 minutes
  10. After the Instapot does a Natural Release, squeeze in the Lemon and stir everything together


Blueberry Oat Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsps. of Ground Flaxseed (if you have regular Flaxseed, use the blender to grind it down)
  • 1½ cups of Rolled Oats, blended into oat flour
  • 1 cup unsweetened Applesauce
  • ¾ cup unsweetened Soy Milk (or any plant milk)
  • 5 Tbsps. of Water
  • 1 tsp. of Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp. of Baking Soda
  • ¼ tsp. of Pink Sea Salt
  • ½ cup of Blueberries (fresh or frozen)
  • Strawberry Preserves

Air Fryer Instructions

  1. Mix ground flaxseed with water and let sit 5 minutes to gel
  2. Combine the gel with all the other ingredients
  3. Pour mixture into muffin holders
  4. AirFry on 325°F for 16 minutes
  5. Let cool for 5-10 minutes before serving
  6. Servce with Strawberry Preserves
